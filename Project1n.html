Cambridge IGCSE


Now, IGCSE can be divided into three main types but here in Star Reachers we use the Cambridge curriculum. The only main differences is that the exams are set by the CAIE and candidates may be given the ICE for academic achievements.The ICE (International Certificate of Education) is given to candidates who passes the following groups.



Group 1 Languages
Group 2 Humanities and Social Sciences
Group 3 Sciences
Group 4 Mathematics
Group 5 Creative and Proffesionals

Grading and Tests


Most IGCSE subjects offer a choice of tiered examinations: Core or Extended papers (in Cambridge). This is designed to make IGCSE suitable for students with varying levels of ability. In some subjects, IGCSE can be taken with or without coursework.The IGCSE exams are graded using the following grades:


A* 90% and above
A 80% and above
B 70% and above
C 60% and above
D 50% and above
F 40% and below

Recognition


The IGCSE prepares students for further academic study, including progression to A Level and BTEC Level 3 study, Cambridge Pre-U, IB Diploma Programme and other equivalents. It is recognised by academic institutions and employers around the world and is considered by many institutions as equivalent to the standard GCSE.

THE CBSE CURRICULUM

CBSE stands for Central Board of Secondary Education and is a board offrered by Star Reachers from Grade 1.

The CBSE exams


CBSE is a national level board of education in India , usually controlled and managed by the Union Government of India. CBSE usually conducts their final board papers of grade 10 and 12 during the month of March. In order for promotion , children must be able to gain a minimum of 33% in their overall papers in both 10 and 12. if a child has failed in their subjects they will need to rewrite their papers in the following year.The grades recieved for the CBSE papers usually change over the years and will surely be emailed to parents.The languages taught to chldren are mainly English and Hindi , but it severly depends upon location


Why did we choose CBSE?


CBSE includes several advantages including :
1. CBSE is a student-friendly board
2. CBSE exams are designed to test on the understanding of students
3. CBSE ensures that students experience physical and mental growth in their knowledge
4. It allows kids to pursue their field of interest without enforcing compulsion
5. CBSE sets papers based on topics appropriate to students age


Recognition


CBSE allows students to apply for the best medical colleges in India as well as the top IT courses. Over the years CBSE has conducted the NTA , National Eligibility Test and JEE exams in order to ensure your child recieves the best from their hardwork as well as accomplishing their beloved interests
